More than just code.

I believe in technology as a tool for empowerment. My journey is defined by curiosity, craftsmanship, and continuous learning.

The Backstory

My interest in computers started when I modified a game script to make my character walk faster. That small "aha!" moment—realizing I could influence the digital world—sparked a lifelong passion.

Since then, I've traversed the landscape of data science and digital design, from analyzing complex datasets to crafting intuitive, pixel-perfect user experiences for web and print.

I don't just write code; I think about systems. I care deeply about how software feels, how it scales, and how it serves the humans on the other side of the screen.

Philosophy

Simplicity is key. Complexity breeds bugs and confusion. I strive to find the simplest, most elegant solution to any problem, even if it takes more effort to find it.
